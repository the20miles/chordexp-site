Features

Everything a musician needs

One app for your entire musical workflow — from writing down a chord progression to walking on stage.

AI & OCR Import

Paste a song's text and AI automatically detects the chords. Or upload a photo of handwritten sheet music with OCR import — no typing required.

Instant Transposition

Transpose any song to any key in one tap. All chords update automatically — perfect for every voice or instrument.

Tags & Smart Filters

Label songs with custom tags and filter your library in one tap — by genre, occasion, key or any criteria you need on the spot. Find the right song in seconds.

Song Photos

Have notes on paper? Attach photos directly to a song. All your visual material — handwritten charts, printed scores, annotations — always within reach.

Setlist Builder

Build the perfect setlist for your gig. Drag, reorder, and share it with your band before the show. Auto-archives after the event date.

Band Collaboration

Create your band, invite members via link and share songs and setlists in real time. Everyone always in sync — no more PDFs on WhatsApp.

In depth

Built for real musicians

Every feature was designed around what you actually need on stage, in rehearsal, or at home.

✦ Chord viewer

See chords and lyrics, perfectly aligned

ChordExp displays your song exactly as you need it — chords positioned above the right syllable, with the ability to transpose on the fly without losing your place.

  • Chords displayed inline above lyrics
  • Instant key transposition — semitone by semitone
  • Fullscreen mode for stage use
  • Supports complex chords: Cmaj7, Am/E, Bsus4…
